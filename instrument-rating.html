Instrument Rating

Fly by reference to instruments alone, through cloud and weather that keeps VFR pilots grounded, trained in one of the busiest airspace environments in the country.

Request informationCall 773 284-5678

An Instrument Rating lets you fly in clouds and low visibility under Instrument Flight Rules (IFR), the capability that turns a good-weather hobby into dependable transportation and the next essential step toward a flying career. At API you earn it where it matters: flying real approaches with real ATC at Chicago Midway, not in a quiet practice area.

We teach it through Instrument Attitude Flying, with a refined syllabus that exists nowhere else. You'll learn precise instrument scan and control built on the same horizon-first foundation, so flying the gauges feels like an extension of what you already know.
