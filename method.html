The API Method

Attitude Flying

Our copyrighted, horizon-first method — the reason API students solo confident and fly safe.

Request informationCall 773 284-5678

Most schools bury new students in instruments. We start with the one reference that never lies — the relationship between your aircraft and the horizon.

By learning to read the visual picture out the window first, you control altitude, bank, coordination, and airspeed by sight before you ever chase a needle. It's faster to learn, it sticks, and it builds the instinct that keeps pilots ahead of the airplane — the same instinct that carries straight into instrument flying.

It's our own copyrighted technique, taught only at API and refined over fifty years and thousands of students at Chicago Midway.
