Private Pilot Certificate

The license to fly an airplane and carry passengers, the foundation every other rating is built on. Earn it at Chicago Midway with one instructor, one student, and the Attitude Flying method.

Request informationCall 773 284-5678

The Private Pilot Certificate (PPL) is where most people begin and where most fall in love with flying. It lets you act as pilot in command of a single-engine airplane, carry passengers, fly day or night, and travel across the country. At Aviation Professionals, you train one-on-one at Chicago Midway International Airport (MDW), real Class C airspace where talking to a control tower and flying a busy environment becomes second nature, so you can fly confidently anywhere.

We teach the Private through Attitude Flying, our copyrighted, horizon-first method: you learn to control altitude, bank, and airspeed by the visual picture out the window before you ever chase an instrument. It clicks faster and it sticks. And because we're family-run and open seven days a week, you pay as you fly, no contracts, no membership dues, paced entirely to your schedule.
