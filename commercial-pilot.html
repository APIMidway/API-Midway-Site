Commercial Pilot & the road to the airlines

The certificate that lets you be paid to fly, and the turning point where flying becomes a career.

Request informationCall 773 284-5678

The Commercial Pilot Certificate raises your flying to professional standards and earns you the right to be compensated as a pilot. For most students it's the gateway to building hours, instructing, and ultimately reaching the airlines. API has trained Chicago pilots on this path for fifty years, and our chief pilot was named the FAA Great Lakes Region Flight Instructor of the Year in 2020.
