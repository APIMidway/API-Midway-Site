The API Method

Attitude Flying

Most schools bury new students in instruments. We start with the one reference that never lies — the relationship between your aircraft and the horizon.

By learning to read the visual picture out the window first, you control altitude, bank, coordination, and airspeed by sight before you ever chase a needle. It's faster to learn, it sticks, and it builds the instinct that keeps pilots ahead of the airplane.

It's our own copyrighted technique, and it's the reason API students solo confident and fly safe.
